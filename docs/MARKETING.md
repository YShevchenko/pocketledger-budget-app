# PocketLedger Marketing Strategy

**Document:** MARKETING.md  
**Product:** PocketLedger (Budget App)  

## 1. Viability Classification
**Tier 3: Danger Zone**  
Finance app CPIs average over $8.00. Broad marketing will bleed money.

## 2. Target Audience & Ad Platforms
- **Primary Platform:** Organic TikTok, Apple Search Ads (long-tail keywords). *Avoid broad Facebook Finance ads.*
- **Targeting:** Dave Ramsey followers, YNAB churners, extreme privacy advocates.

## 3. Core Marketing Hook (Ad Copy)
"Automated bank syncing makes you lazy and sells your data to credit card companies. Take back control with manual envelope budgeting. No Plaid. No $100/yr fees. 100% private."

## 4. Geographic & Pricing Strategy
- **Tier 2 (EU):** Focus on Germany and Switzerland where Plaid/Open Banking is viewed with suspicion. Highlight "100% Local SQLite Database" heavily in App Store screenshots.
