## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

💰 **PocketLedger — The Fastest Way to Track Your Budget**

Tired of budget apps that sync with your bank but never quite get it right? PocketLedger puts you in control with the classic envelope budgeting method — simple, powerful, and 100% offline.

---

🚀 **Giant Numpad for Lightning-Fast Entry**

The centerpiece of PocketLedger is its oversized numpad — designed for one-handed use, even on the go. Log a coffee, grocery run, or rent payment in under 3 seconds. No accounts, no sync, no friction.

---

📂 **Envelope Budgeting That Actually Works**

Divide your monthly income into spending envelopes — Groceries, Rent, Entertainment, Savings — and watch your money stay on track. When an envelope hits zero, you know it's time to stop or transfer.

- Create unlimited custom envelopes
- Set monthly budget limits per envelope
- Color-code envelopes for instant visual scanning
- See your total budget, total spent, and total remaining at a glance

---

📊 **Smart Dashboard**

Your financial picture, front and center:
- Balance bar showing budget health across all envelopes
- Month selector to review any past month
- Overspent envelopes highlighted in red
- Savings rate calculated automatically

---

🔄 **Recurring Bills — Set It and Forget It**

Add your regular expenses (rent, subscriptions, utilities) as recurring bills. PocketLedger automatically logs them to the right envelope each month, so you're never caught off guard.

---

↔️ **Envelope Transfers**

Need to move money from Entertainment to Groceries mid-month? One-tap transfers keep your budget flexible without losing visibility.

---

📋 **Full Transaction Ledger**

Browse your complete spending history:
- Filter by envelope or month
- Search by merchant or note
- Edit or delete any transaction

---

📤 **Export Your Data**

Your data belongs to you. Export to:
- **CSV** — for Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app
- **JSON** — for full data portability and backup

---

🔒 **Privacy First**

- **No account required** — ever
- **No bank connection** — your credentials never leave your device
- **No cloud sync** — data stored locally in SQLite
- **No tracking** — we don't collect personal financial data

---

📅 **Monthly Resets**

At the start of each month, envelope balances reset so you start fresh. Historical data stays intact so you can compare months and spot trends.

---

✨ **Clean, Modern Design**

PocketLedger is built with Material Design 3 principles — readable typography, accessible colors, dark mode support.

---

**Who is PocketLedger for?**

- Anyone who wants to budget without linking bank accounts
- Couples managing shared household finances
- Cash-heavy budgeters using the envelope method
- People escaping subscription-based finance apps
