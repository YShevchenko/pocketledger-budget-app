## Description (max 4000 chars)

PocketLedger is the fastest way to track your personal budget using the proven envelope method — no bank accounts, no subscriptions, no cloud required.

---

GIANT NUMPAD FOR 3-SECOND ENTRY

The oversized numpad is purpose-built for speed. Tap your amount, pick an envelope, done. Log transactions faster than opening a Notes app. Works one-handed in your pocket or bag.

---

ENVELOPE BUDGETING THAT MAKES SENSE

Divide your monthly income into spending buckets — Groceries, Rent, Entertainment, Gas, Savings — and watch your money stay where it belongs.

- Create unlimited custom envelopes with your own names and colors
- Set a monthly budget amount per envelope
- See spent vs. remaining instantly on every envelope card
- Overspent envelopes surface immediately with a red highlight

---

SMART DASHBOARD

Your full financial picture at a glance:
- Visual balance bar showing overall budget health
- Month-by-month navigation to review any past period
- Savings rate calculated automatically from your data
- Total budget, total spent, total remaining — always visible

---

RECURRING BILLS

Add rent, subscriptions, and utilities as recurring bills. PocketLedger books them automatically to the right envelope each month on the day they're due — no manual entry needed.

---

ENVELOPE TRANSFERS

Move money between envelopes mid-month when life changes your plans. Full transfer history is preserved so your records always add up.

---

TRANSACTION LEDGER

Your complete spending history in one scrollable list:
- Filter by envelope or by month
- Edit or delete any entry
- Notes and merchant names supported

---

EXPORT YOUR DATA

Everything you enter is yours. Export to CSV for spreadsheets or JSON for backup — anytime, no restrictions, no paywalls.

---

PRIVACY FIRST

PocketLedger never asks for a bank login, never syncs to the cloud, and never requires an account. All data lives in an encrypted database on your device.

- No account or sign-in
- No bank or credit card connection
- No analytics or behavioral tracking
- No subscription required for core features

---

CLEAN, READABLE DESIGN

Built on Material Design 3 with large readable fonts, high contrast colors, and full dark mode support. Designed to be usable without squinting.
